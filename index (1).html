Investment Highlights

A rare institutional opportunity on Ireland's premier retail street

The Grafton Collection comprises four adjoining buildings at 21–23 Grafton Street and 24 Duke Street, together with Creation Arcade at 6–7 Duke Street — collectively offering a significant foothold in Dublin's most tightly-held and supply-constrained retail market.

The collection is offered for sale in individual or collective lots by Hines on behalf of its Pan-European Core Fund (GECF), with tenants unaffected.

Location

The epicentre of
Dublin's retail core

The Grafton Collection occupies a pivotal position on Grafton Street in Dublin 2 — Ireland's most prestigious and heavily-trafficked retail thoroughfare, entirely pedestrianised and running from St Stephen's Green to College Green.

The street is home to premium international brands including Brown Thomas, Weirs, Ted Baker, Hugo Boss, Massimo Dutti, Marks & Spencer and River Island. With vacancy effectively at zero, competitive bidding among retailers seeking a pitch on Grafton Street remains the defining characteristic of this market.

  • 2 min walk to LUAS Green Line — St Stephen's Green station
  • 4 min walk to DART — Pearse Street Station
  • Adjacent to Dawson Street, Duke Street and Anne Street South
  • Trinity College Dublin — 5 min walk
  • 500,000+ weekly pedestrian footfall — Ireland's highest
  • Zoned Z5 City Centre — Dublin City Development Plan
